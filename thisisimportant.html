Pursuing the Extraordinary for the sake of human kind


A creative self navigating the world of coding! A Beginner's Experience!

Python, HTML, CSS, Responsive Websites, flexbox, lists, anchor tags, clickable link, organic design,
open source, to name a few, are new language expressions emerging from the experience! It has become
an indelible creative imprint with the power of braking the 'old' code in 'me' and make a new one!

Whether with art, photography, history and writing, the journey begins with an exploration of how
one responds to the learning process of coding! in the context of "learning as you Go".

This has been a tough experience ... as I am not generation X, I am pleased to have been able to make
a website of my own! the process has been that of getting to know better where my limitations are in
terms of technology! learning to 'code' and whilst doing the 'coding' has been an eye opener for a
world of possibilities! it has equipped me with new perspectives and has expanded my knowledge base to
work more effectively.

In a mentor's role

I find inspiration in the work mentees do. Currently I provide support to a group of women of hispanic
descend who are setting up a website to have presence on the web! The experience is equally valuable
for mentoring studens in their last year of their degree whilst in the process of thinking about what to write
their dissertations about. Coding offers a platform to think and do things differently!

Transferable Skills

From a psychological and psychotherapeutic perspectives, having been introduced to the field of Coding seems
so natural to me ... skills like analytical thinking, critical thinking, creaivity, Information management,
judgements and decision making, adaptability, integrity, self-regulation, collaboration, inclusivity, leadership,
management, service orientation, flexibility/adaptability to new systems, to name a few!

Learning to code with Python is a skill everybody should have! it is a very useful tool based on the 'give and take'
communication system, offering plenty of possibilities, for example: the development of neurological nurturing
and neurological nourishment programs targetting children's character development!
